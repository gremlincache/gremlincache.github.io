Coliseum Tracker

A Tampermonkey userscript that tracks your coliseum loot, generates BBCode loot posts, and lets you set up drop goals as quests. No automation of gameplay or scraping. The tool only displays information the browser already sends.

Find the best venues to grind for any coliseum item. Enemy data and pack data based on information by Maki, cataloguing of items, item drop sources and drop rates by me.
